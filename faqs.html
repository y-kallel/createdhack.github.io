Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas and passion into real projects. We’ll provide mentorship and workshops to help you get started!

Can I work in a team?

If you choose to work in a team you are more than welcome to, but you also have the opportunity to participate on your own! We will also hold a team formation event for hackers to find any team members before the start of the hackathon as well. If you already have team members in mind, you’re all set to join!

What can I build?

Anything! As long as it follows our guidelines and can be submitted or demoed virtually!

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem! We encourage people of all experience levels to participate and learn something new. If you want to learn how to code or other specific technical skills, you can join one of our planned workshops. Details TBD.

Does my project have to be themed around social justice?

This hackathon is centered around encouraging innovative solutions to social and environmental issues, but it is not required to follow this theme and you are open to submit any sort of project you create. The challenge categories however, which will include prizes, will revolve around the theme, so hackers whose projects correspond with the theme best will have a greater chance at winning challenges!

Do I have to be a CDMHS student to participate?

No! We encourage any highschool students graduating in 2021+ to participate. The hackathon will be fully remote and will not require any travelling either!

Who can attend?

Any current high school student graduating in 2021+ can attend.

Do you have a code of conduct?

Yes, we will be following the MLH Code of Conduct.
TLDR: Be respectful and kind.

Have some other questions?

Feel free to email us or reach out to any of our other social media profiles for any questions you might still have.
