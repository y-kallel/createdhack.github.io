About

HackCDM is a 24-hour hackathon run by high school students for high school students. Project themes are centered on social justice issues and using technology to address them.

HackCDM is open to any high school student graduating in or before 2021 and with any level of experience or lack thereof.

As part of Major League Hacking, we will be following MLH's Code of Conduct.

Applications are now open. Click on "apply" at the top. Also make sure to check out our sponsors who are making this event possible! They're all hiring and it's a perfect time to look for an internship. Have a question? We have an FAQ!
